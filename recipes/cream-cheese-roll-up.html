Cream Cheese Roll Up

Description

Cream cheese roll ups make for a fun, simlpe dessert to make that everyone will enjoy! With just five ingredients and a little time you can enjoy a tasty snack within half an hour!

Ingreidents

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cream Cheese
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Prepare bread by slicing off the crusts
  3. In one bowl prepare a mixture of cinnamon and sugar
  4. Heat the butter in a seperate bowl
  5. Dip each slice of bread first in the butter, then in your cinnamon-sugar mixture
  6. Cover each slice with a layer of cream cheese, then roll them up and place on a baking sheet
  7. Cook for 20 minutes or until the outsides are golden brown
