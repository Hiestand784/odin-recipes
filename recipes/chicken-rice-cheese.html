Chicken Cheese and Rice

Description

Got a crockpot? Toss in these three easy ingredients for a tasty meal when you come back home!

Ingreidents

  • Chicken
  • Cheese
  • Rice

Directions

  1. Add pre-cookced rice to your crockpot
  2. Insert chicken breast cut into slices or chunks
  3. Top with cheese and any other desired toppings
  4. Cook on low for 6 hours
